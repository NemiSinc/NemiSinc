Biography

Nermin Sinclair
Engineer & Data Analyst

Nermin Sinclair was born and raised in Germany and moved to America in 2016. She holds a bachelor's degree in electrical engineering and computer science, specializing in medical technology. Currently, she is pursuing a master's degree in business data analytics from West Virginia University and will graduate in August 2022. Her bachelor thesis builds of the Eulerian Video Magnification concept from MIT, where she used object-oriented programming with C++. She developed a software program with a GUI. The program amplified invisible movement changes in video sequences and made them visible for the naked eye. The program allows users to measure heart or pulse rate via video sequences in real-time or asynchronous. 

Since her childhood, she has always loved math and was able to recognize patterns between different problems. When she was 13 years old, she developed a strategy how to solve any sliding puzzle. Nermin has a great interest in data analytics because it combines algorithms and coding. She said, "Data Analytics is like solving a puzzle. The numbers are the puzzle pieces, and we have to put them together to see the whole picture to tell a story!".
